Euchre

Euchre is a game played in two teams of 1 or 2 players, using the top 24 or 32 cards of a standard deck, where each player receives 5 or 7 cards. The players can select the top card from the deck or offer a different suit as the 'trump'. Once the trump suit has been selected, the Jack of that suit has the highest value, followed by the Jack of the same colour, then the rest of the trump suit in descending order. This project consists of a C# front-end that relies on a Node.js & Express.io back-end.
